What is TMIY?

An Interactive Men's Program
Built for the Modern World

That Man Is You! is a Catholic men's program focused on the development of male leadership in the modern world. It combines the best research from science with the teachings of the Catholic faith and the wisdom of the saints — developing a vision of authentic men capable of transforming themselves, their families, and greater society.

Journey from the birth of the early Church to the call of Christ that echoes in our own day. From Christ to the Apostles, from the Apostolic Fathers to the men of our time, the voice of God has never fallen silent. Now the call is ours to answer.
